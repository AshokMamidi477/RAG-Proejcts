ACME SAAS PLATFORM — CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE

PRICING AND PLANS:
Starter Plan: $29/month — up to 5 users, 10GB storage, email support only.
Professional Plan: $99/month — up to 25 users, 100GB storage, priority email + chat support.
Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing — unlimited users, unlimited storage, 24/7 phone support, dedicated CSM.
All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required for trial.
Annual billing available with 20% discount on all plans.

CANCELLATION AND REFUNDS:
You can cancel your subscription at any time from Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
We do not offer refunds for partial months. Annual subscribers can request a pro-rata refund
within 30 days of renewal if they have not used the service.

INTEGRATIONS:
Native integrations available: Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Salesforce, HubSpot.
API access available on Professional and Enterprise plans.
Zapier integration available on all paid plans.
Webhooks: available on Professional and Enterprise plans.

ACCOUNT AND SECURITY:
Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and recommended for all users.
SSO (SAML 2.0) is available on Enterprise plan only.
Data export: you can export all your data as CSV or JSON from Account Settings > Data > Export.
Password reset: click "Forgot password" on the login page. Reset link expires in 24 hours.

STORAGE AND DATA:
Storage limits apply to uploaded files only (documents, images). Database records are not counted.
If you exceed your storage limit, you will receive an email warning at 80% and 100% usage.
Exceeding the limit prevents new file uploads until storage is freed or plan is upgraded.
Data is backed up daily. Backups retained for 30 days on all plans.

SUPPORT:
Starter: email support only, response within 2 business days.
Professional: email + live chat, response within 4 business hours.
Enterprise: 24/7 phone + email + chat, dedicated Customer Success Manager.
Status page: status.acmesaas.com for real-time system status.
