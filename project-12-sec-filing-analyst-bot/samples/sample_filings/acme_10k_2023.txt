ACME CORPORATION — ANNUAL REPORT (FORM 10-K) — FISCAL YEAR 2023

PART I — BUSINESS

Acme Corporation is a leading provider of enterprise software solutions serving mid-market
companies across North America and Europe. Founded in 2008, the Company operates through
two segments: SaaS Products (72% of revenue) and Professional Services (28% of revenue).

PART II — FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS

Total revenue for fiscal 2023: $482.3 million (up 18% YoY)
Recurring revenue (ARR): $347 million (up 24% YoY)
Gross margin: 74% (up from 71% in 2022)
Net loss: $23.1 million (improved from $41.2 million loss in 2022)
Cash and equivalents: $218 million

PART I ITEM 1A — RISK FACTORS

Macroeconomic Risk: A prolonged economic downturn could cause customers to reduce or delay
software spending, adversely affecting our revenue and growth rates.

Competition Risk: The enterprise software market is highly competitive. We face competition
from SAP, Oracle, Salesforce and numerous well-funded startups. Some competitors have
significantly greater resources than we do.

Customer Concentration Risk: Our top 10 customers accounted for 31% of total revenue in 2023.
Loss of any major customer could materially impact results.

MANAGEMENT DISCUSSION — OUTLOOK

Management expects fiscal 2024 revenue in the range of $555-570 million (15-18% growth).
The Company plans to increase R&D investment by 22% to accelerate AI feature development.
International expansion into DACH and Nordic markets is planned for H2 2024.
