CLINICAL TRIAL SUMMARY — TRIAL ID: CT-2023-ONC-007

Title: PD-1 Inhibitor Combination Therapy in Advanced Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer

Phase: Phase II
Disease Area: Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC), Stage IIIB/IV
Sponsor: OncoPharma Ltd
Duration: 24 months (ongoing)
Sample Size: 120 participants

PRIMARY ENDPOINT:
Overall response rate (ORR) at 6 months.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Age 18-75
- ECOG performance status 0-2
- No prior PD-1/PD-L1 therapy
- Confirmed NSCLC histology

INTERIM RESULTS (12-month data):
ORR: 41% in combination arm vs 24% in monotherapy arm.
Median progression-free survival: 8.3 months (combination) vs 5.1 months (monotherapy).
Disease control rate: 68% vs 52%.

ADVERSE EVENTS:
Grade 3-4 immune-related adverse events: 18% in combination vs 9% in monotherapy.
Most common: pneumonitis (7%), colitis (5%), hepatitis (3%).

CONCLUSION:
Combination PD-1 inhibitor therapy shows promising efficacy signal with manageable toxicity.
Full 24-month data pending.
