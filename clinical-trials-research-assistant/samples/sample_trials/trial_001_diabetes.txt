CLINICAL TRIAL SUMMARY — TRIAL ID: CT-2023-DM-001

Title: Efficacy of GLP-1 Receptor Agonist vs. Insulin Glargine in Type 2 Diabetes Management

Phase: Phase III
Disease Area: Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
Sponsor: MedResearch Corp
Duration: 52 weeks
Sample Size: 486 participants (243 per arm)

PRIMARY ENDPOINT:
Change in HbA1c from baseline at 52 weeks.

RESULTS:
GLP-1 arm achieved mean HbA1c reduction of 1.8% vs 1.4% for insulin glargine (p=0.003).
Weight loss was significantly greater in GLP-1 arm (-4.2 kg vs +1.1 kg, p<0.001).
Hypoglycaemic events were lower in GLP-1 arm (12% vs 28%).

CONCLUSION:
GLP-1 receptor agonist demonstrated superior glycaemic control and weight benefit with
a more favourable hypoglycaemia profile compared to insulin glargine over 52 weeks.

ADVERSE EVENTS:
Nausea reported in 22% of GLP-1 participants (mostly mild-moderate, resolved within 4 weeks).
No serious cardiac events in either arm.
