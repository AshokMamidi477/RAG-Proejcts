ICD-10-CM DIAGNOSTIC CODE REFERENCE — SELECTED CODES

CHAPTER 9: DISEASES OF THE CIRCULATORY SYSTEM

I10 — Essential (primary) hypertension
High blood pressure with no identifiable cause. Most common form of hypertension.

I20.0 — Unstable angina
Chest pain due to reduced blood flow to the heart. Occurs at rest or with minimal exertion.
Requires urgent evaluation. Includes: crescendo angina, de novo effort angina.

I21.0 — ST elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI) of anterior wall
Heart attack involving the anterior (front) wall. Characterised by ST elevation on ECG.

I21.9 — Acute myocardial infarction, unspecified
Heart attack, type not further specified. Use when documentation is insufficient for specificity.

I25.10 — Atherosclerotic heart disease without angina pectoris
Coronary artery disease with plaque buildup but no angina symptoms documented.

I50.9 — Heart failure, unspecified
Cardiac failure where the heart cannot pump sufficient blood. When type not documented.

CHAPTER 10: DISEASES OF THE RESPIRATORY SYSTEM

J18.9 — Pneumonia, unspecified organism
Lung infection when specific causative organism not identified or documented.

J44.1 — Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease with acute exacerbation
COPD with sudden worsening of symptoms. Requires code for COPD type if known.

J45.20 — Mild intermittent asthma, uncomplicated
Asthma with symptoms fewer than 2 days per week, not affecting normal activity.

CHAPTER 11: DISEASES OF THE DIGESTIVE SYSTEM

K21.0 — Gastro-oesophageal reflux disease with oesophagitis
GORD/GERD with inflammation of the oesophagus. Includes: reflux oesophagitis.

K57.30 — Diverticulosis of large intestine without perforation, abscess, or bleeding
Pouches in the colon wall without complications.

CHAPTER 4: ENDOCRINE, NUTRITIONAL AND METABOLIC DISEASES

E11.9 — Type 2 diabetes mellitus without complications
Most common form of diabetes. Characterised by insulin resistance. No complications documented.

E11.65 — Type 2 diabetes mellitus with hyperglycaemia
Type 2 diabetes with elevated blood glucose levels documented at encounter.

E78.5 — Hyperlipidaemia, unspecified
Elevated lipids in blood. Includes: hypercholesterolaemia, hypertriglyceridaemia.
